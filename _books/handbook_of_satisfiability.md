The reference for everything CDCL, MaxSAT, and proof-system related. Chapters on conflict-driven search and on inprocessing techniques are most relevant to my MWDS / DiverseSAT / NLIP work; the MaxSAT chapter reset my mental model of how PB / WBO / hybrid solvers actually trade off bound quality vs. search effort.
