## Context

Student innovation-entrepreneurship project (大创, **completed 2025-10**). We quantify the therapeutic effect of mindfulness meditation on college student mental health using **EEG wearable devices** (BrainBand / 脑环).

## Method

Collect pre-/post-meditation EEG signals from participants, pair them with standardized mental-health questionnaires, and perform paired statistical analysis to verify the effect of the intervention.

## Contribution

- Designed the full data-collection protocol (consent + wearable setup + meditation guidance).
- Analyzed alpha/beta band power shifts across pre-/post-meditation windows.
- Wrote the final research report; the project was successfully concluded in October 2025.

This applied project complements my theoretical research by grounding algorithmic thinking in human-subject data.
